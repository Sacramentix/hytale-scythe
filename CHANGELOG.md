# Mod Changelog

## v1.1.0

- Add signature ability to scythe. Harvest 100 crop with scythe to gather enough signature energy to launch an harvesting tornado.
- Add durability mechanic lose 1 durability for each 100 crop harvested.
- Make bucket and water bucket stackable up to 100.
- Add debug command /fillenergy to fill signature energy instantly

## v1.0.0

- Add 4 scythe made of iron, thorium, cobalt and adamantite to harvest crop faster
- Add debug command /tillandsow to plant fully grow wheat around the player
